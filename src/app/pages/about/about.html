🌟 Our Story

Built for Every
Student in India

TOPIQ was founded with one powerful belief: every student in India, regardless of background, deserves access to world-class exam preparation. We saw a gap — talented students in tier-2 and tier-3 cities were being left behind simply because of resource limitations.

Today, TOPIQ serves over 2 lakh students across 25+ Indian cities with a mobile-first learning platform that delivers daily challenges, expert content, live leaderboards, and real scholarship rewards. We believe learning should be engaging, competitive, and rewarding.

🔭 Direction & Purpose

Vision & Mission

🔭

Our Vision

To become India's most impactful educational technology platform — creating a generation of empowered, knowledgeable, and self-confident learners who can compete on any national stage.

We envision a future where every child in India — from a village school to an urban academy — has equal access to quality education, fair competition, and merit-based rewards.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide affordable, engaging, and outcome-driven exam preparation resources that transform how Indian students learn, practice, and grow academically.

Through daily challenges, leaderboards, scholarships, and mentor-guided content, we make rigorous exam preparation feel achievable, motivating, and worthwhile.

💡

Innovation First

We continuously push the boundaries of EdTech — incorporating AI-powered personalization, gamified learning mechanics, and real-time competitive analytics.

Our platform evolves every month based on student feedback, educator insights, and the latest developments in pedagogy and technology.

🤝

Community & Trust

TOPIQ is more than a platform — it's a community of achievers. We foster peer learning, healthy competition, and mentor-student relationships that last lifetimes.

Our transparent reward system, verified scholarships, and open ranking mechanisms ensure every student trusts and believes in the platform.
